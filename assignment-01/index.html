Top 5 Metal Bands

  1. Black Sabbath

    The originators of heavy metal. Their dark riffs and lyrics defined the genre.

  2. Metallica

    The kings of thrash metal and one of the most commercially successful metal bands ever.

  3. Iron Maiden

    Epic storytelling, twin guitar harmonies, and Bruce Dickinson's soaring vocals.

  4. Slayer

    Speed, aggression, and intensity; essential in shaping extreme metal.

  5. Pantera

    Groove metal pioneers who revitalized heavy music in the '90s with crushing riffs and attitude.

This list highlights the five most influential and defining bands in heavy metal history, focusing on influence, musicianship, and legacy across metal's subgenres.
