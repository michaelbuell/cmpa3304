My Collection

One of my biggest interests is music and I am interested in lots of different genres. My favorite metal band is Metallica. This photo collection showcases a few photos of Metallica Performing live. Metallica is a hugely influential American heavy metal band, often credited as one of the “Big Four” of thrash metal (alongside Slayer, Megadeth, and Anthrax).

James and Kirk

Photo of James Hetfield and Kirk Hammett of Metallica playing live.

This is James Hetfield(left) and Kirk Hammett(right). James is the lead vocalist, rhythm guitarist, co-founder, and principal songwriter of Metallica. He co-founded Metallica in 1981 with drummer Lars Ulrich. Kirk has been the lead guitarist for Metallica since 1983.

The Full Band

Photo of all 4 members of Metallica

Metallica formed in 1981 in Los Angeles, but spent much of their career based in San Francisco. They are known for fast tempos, aggressive musicianship, and powerful instrumentals.

James and Kirk

Photo of James Hetfield and Kirk Hammett of Metallica.

An interesting fact about Metallica is that they were the first band to play on all seven continents, including performing a special, quiet, science-friendly show in Antarctica in 2013—earning them a Guinness World Record.

James Hetfield

Photo of James Hetfield of Metallica

Here is a shot of James Hetfield performing passionately for the audience. One of my favorite things about Metallica, and something I look for in the bands I listen to, is that they are great performers live. Some might even say they sound better live than their recorded music.
